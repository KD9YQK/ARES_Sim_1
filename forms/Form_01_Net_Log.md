## Priority Labels

Routine — ordinary status or administrative traffic.  
Priority — affects safety, movement, resources, or current situation.  
Emergency Simulation — urgent simulated life-safety traffic.  
Real-World Emergency — actual emergency traffic; pause the exercise.

## Notes

Use concise summaries. Do not attempt to transcribe every word unless required by the exercise.
