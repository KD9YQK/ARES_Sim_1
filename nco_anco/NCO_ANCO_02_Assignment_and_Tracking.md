## Information Confidence Labels

Use simple labels to avoid confusion.

Confirmed:

Information directly observed by a team or confirmed by GM.

Unconfirmed:

Reported by a third party, resident, or other source but not verified.

Conflicting:

Two or more reports disagree.

Resolved:

GM has provided closure or the issue has been superseded.

Pending:

NCO has assigned or requested action, but GM has not provided the result yet.

## Suggested ANCO Workflow

1. Record check-ins and starting locations.
2. Mark Rescue Bravo as enroute until 00:10 arrival report.
3. Mark hazards clearly and visibly.
4. Keep a separate list of unresolved items.
5. When NCO assigns a task, mark it as pending.
6. Ask GM for results when needed.
7. At 00:30 and 00:45, provide NCO with a concise status summary if requested.
8. At 00:59, prepare final unresolved items for debrief.

## Tracking Tips

Write times for every major report.

Do not erase uncertainty. Change “missing” to “conflicting/unconfirmed,” not “resolved,” unless GM confirms it.

If NCO gives an instruction to a team, track it as pending until GM gives a result.

If a team reports “unknown,” write unknown. Do not fill in blanks.
