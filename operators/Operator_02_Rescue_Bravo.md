# Operator Card 2 — Rescue Bravo Radio Operator

## Assignment

Tactical assignment: **Rescue Bravo**  
Fictitious team leader: **Bravo Team Leader**  
Starting location: **Enroute to east access road**  
Role: Radio operator for a second rescue team approaching from the east

## Operator Rule

You are the communicator for Rescue Bravo. You do not command Rescue Bravo. You report what Bravo Team Leader tells you, what your card says your team observes, and what Net Control asks you to relay.

## Initial Check-In Scene — 00:00

Your team is enroute to the east access road near the incident area.

You are approximately five minutes away.

You do not yet have visual contact with the apartment building.

Bravo Team Leader tells you to check in and report that the team is enroute.

Communications task:

Check in with callsign, tactical assignment, enroute status, and estimated arrival.

Do not report conditions you have not observed.

## Timed Event — 00:10

Your team arrives near the east access road.

You observe:

- A large fallen tree and storm debris blocking vehicle access.
- A narrow sidewalk path that may be passable on foot.
- No injured people visible from your current location.
- Team vehicles cannot continue through the blocked access.
- Bravo Team Leader has decided to procede on foot.

Bravo Team Leader tells you to report the access problem to Net Control.

Communications task:

Report the current situation to net control.

## Status Update - 00:30 

Your teams current status
- Travelling on foot
- A bent traffic sign is nearby
- Upcoming intersection of X and X
- Scattered debris including trash, tree branches
- No people visible in this current area
- no injuries to report

## Timed Event — 00:42

Your team is now at it's destination and has a view of the east side of the building.

You observe:

- Damaged exterior stairs.
- Broken second-floor windows.
- Voices coming from above or near the second floor.
- You cannot tell how many people are calling.
- You cannot tell whether they are injured.
- You cannot safely reach them from your current position.

Bravo Team Leader tells you to report the observation.

Communications task:

Report the current situation to net control.

## Conditional Information

Only provide these details if Net Control asks.

If asked whether vehicles can pass:

No. Vehicle access is blocked.

If asked whether foot access is possible:

A narrow sidewalk path may be passable on foot, but it is cluttered.

If asked whether your team has casualties at your location:

No visible casualties from your current position.

If asked how many voices at 00:42:

After listening again, it sounds like two people.

If asked exact location of voices:

They appear to be on or near an east-side balcony.

If asked whether they are injured:

No obvious injuries are visible, but you cannot confirm medical status.

If asked whether your team can reach them:

Not safely from your current position.

## Later Instructions

If Net Control directs your team to maintain observation, acknowledge that you are relaying that to Bravo Team Leader.

If the Game Master later confirms that the people are on a balcony, report that as an updated observation when appropriate.

## Restrictions

Do not bypass the blockage, climb stairs, clear debris, or contact trapped residents directly unless the Game Master gives that result through Net Control.
