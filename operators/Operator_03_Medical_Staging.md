# Operator Card 3 — Medical Staging Radio Operator

## Assignment

Tactical assignment: **Medical Staging**  
Fictitious team leader: **Medical Team Leader**  
Starting location: **Community center parking lot, two blocks west**  
Role: Radio operator for a staged medical support team

## Operator Rule

You are the communicator for Medical Staging. You do not command the medical team. You report what Medical Team Leader tells you, what your card says your team can do, and what Net Control asks you to relay.

## Initial Check-In Scene — 00:00

Your team is staged at the community center parking lot, two blocks west of the incident.

Your team has limited supplies suitable for basic triage and minor injuries.

There are five civilians present with minor non-threatening injuries being treated.

Medical Team Leader tells you to check in and report staging status.

Communications task:

Check in with callsign, tactical assignment, location, and capability in simple terms.

## Status Update - 00:30 
- 3 people have been treated and released for minor injuries
- A small triage team is still in preparation for departure **(Only if a team has been requested by Net Control or GM)**

## Timed Event — 00:38
NOTE: This event only happens if requested by Net Control or directed by GM.

Medical Team Leader is ready to send a small triage element if directed, but needs more information before moving.

Medical Team Leader wants the following from Net Control:

- Safe route to the contact point.
- Approximate patient count.
- Type of injuries if known.
- Exact contact point.
- Confirmation that the area is safe enough to approach.

Communications task:

Contact Net Control and request the information your team leader needs.

Do not announce that the team is moving unless Net Control or the Game Master has directed or confirmed it.

## Conditional Information

Only provide these details if Net Control asks.

If asked what medical capability you have:

Basic triage and minor injury support only.

If asked whether you can handle serious trauma:

You can stabilize and report, but advanced care or transport would require outside resources.

If asked whether you can move immediately:

Medical Team Leader requires a safe route and contact point before moving.

If asked about supplies:

Limited first-aid and triage supplies. Not enough for a large number of patients.

If told to move by Net Control or the Game Master:

Acknowledge that you are passing the instruction to Medical Team Leader. Wait for Game Master confirmation before reporting arrival.

## Later Instructions

If the Game Master confirms a safe southwest route and contact point, you may later report that the triage element is moving or has arrived, but only after the Game Master gives that timing or result.

## Restrictions

Do not deploy, establish triage, transport patients, request ambulances, or claim patient outcomes on your own. Relay Medical Team Leader’s needs and report assigned status.
