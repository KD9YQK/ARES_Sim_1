# Operator Card 5 — Shelter / Resident Accountability Radio Operator

## Assignment

Tactical assignment: **Shelter**  
Fictitious team leader: **Shelter Lead**  
Starting location: **Church basement on 5th Street**  
Role: Radio operator for a resident reception and accountability site

## Operator Rule

You are the communicator for Shelter. You do not command the shelter operation. You report what Shelter Lead tells you, what residents report to your team, and what Net Control asks you to relay.

## Initial Check-In Scene — 00:00

Your team is at the church basement on 5th Street.

The site is being prepared to receive displaced residents.

No residents from the apartment building have arrived yet.

Shelter Lead tells you to check in and report shelter status.

Communications task:

Check in with callsign, tactical assignment, location, and readiness.

## Timed Event — 00:30, During Roll Call

During the 00:30 roll call and location update, several residents arrive at the shelter.

You learn:

- Eight residents from the damaged apartment building have arrived.
- One resident says an elderly neighbor from apartment 3B may still be inside.
- The resident is upset and unsure.
- Another resident says they think that same person left earlier.
- The information is conflicting and unconfirmed.

Shelter Lead tells you this may need to be passed to Net Control.

Communications task:

Decide how to report this to Net Control.

NOTE: This is the one operator card where you NEED to break into the roll call with priority traffic.  You are simulating a break in regular traffic, and add an element of stress to Net Control.

## Timed Event — 00:55

More residents have arrived at the shelter.

You learn:

- There are now 17 residents at the shelter.
- A newly arrived resident states that the apartment 3B occupant left with family before the collapse.

Shelter Lead tells you to update Net Control.

## Conditional Information

Only provide these details if Net Control asks.

If asked who reported apartment 3B:

A displaced resident at the shelter. You do not have independent confirmation.

If asked whether the resident is definitely missing:

No. The information is conflicting.

If asked how many residents are at shelter at 00:30:

Eight.

If asked how many residents are at shelter at 00:55:

Seventeen.

If asked whether shelter needs supplies:

No immediate supply request unless the Game Master gives one.

If asked whether shelter can receive more residents:

Yes, the site can receive more residents for the purpose of the exercise.

## Later Instructions

If Net Control asks you to continue collecting names or apartment numbers, acknowledge that you are relaying that to Shelter Lead. Wait for the Game Master before producing new numbers or lists.

## Restrictions

Do not declare anyone missing, rescued, dead, safe, or accounted for unless your card or the Game Master says so. You only report what residents told your team and whether it is confirmed or unconfirmed.
